Shaocong Wang

Shaocong Wang is a final year PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Zhongrui Wang.

His research interests includes AI hardware acceleration, software hardware co-design, resistive memory, computing-in-memory, AI for microelectronics, and AI for physics.
